Keynotif watches your notifications while you sleep. When you wake up and open the app, you don't get a flood. You get a **digest** — a calm, clean summary of everything that happened overnight, filtered down to what's actually worth your attention.

One screen. Thirty seconds. Done.

## Known Limitations

**OEM Battery Optimization**
Manufacturers like Xiaomi, OPPO, Realme, and Vivo aggressively kill background services. Keynotif's onboarding includes device-specific instructions to add it to the battery optimization whitelist. Without this, the service may not survive overnight on some devices.

**Notification Content Access**
Some apps (notably banking and secure messengers) restrict notification content from being read by listener services. For these, Keynotif will capture that a notification arrived but may not be able to surface the content.

**No iOS Support**
iOS does not expose a notification listener API to third-party apps. Keynotif is Android-only by necessity, not by choice.
