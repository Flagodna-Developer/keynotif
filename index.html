How it works

Three steps to a calmer morning.

Keynotif runs quietly in the background so you don't have to manage it. You just wake up, open the app, and know what needs your attention.

1
It collects while you sleep

Keynotif runs quietly in the background after your bedtime — capturing notifications from every app, never waking you. Your sleep stays intact.

2
AI summarizes them privately

In the morning, your notifications are anonymized first — emails, numbers, and links replaced with placeholders — then sent to AI for summarization. Your raw data never leaves protected.

3
Read it or just listen

Open the app and read your digest, or tap play and let text-to-speech read it aloud while you make coffee. One minute. Full picture. Ready to start.

Design principles

Built with intention.

Every decision in Keynotif was made to protect your attention, not monetize it.

🎯
Signal over noise

Not all notifications deserve equal weight. Keynotif treats them differently — surfacing what needs you, silencing what doesn't.

🌅
Your morning is sacred

The first minutes of your day set the tone for everything that follows. Keynotif guards them so you start on your terms, not someone else's.

🔒
Privacy without compromise

Before AI ever sees your data, sensitive content is automatically anonymized — emails, phone numbers, OTPs, and links replaced with placeholders. Your raw notifications stay on-device.

🎛️
You define what matters

Set your VIP contacts, mute categories, and choose your digest time. Keynotif adapts to your life — not the other way around.

Known limitations

Honest about the edges.

Some things are outside our control. We'd rather you know upfront than be surprised later.

🔋
Aggressive battery optimization (Xiaomi, OPPO, Realme, Vivo)

Many Android manufacturers aggressively terminate background services to save battery. Keynotif includes step-by-step onboarding to whitelist the app — but without this step, overnight listening may not work reliably on these devices.

🔐
Restricted notification content

Certain apps — particularly banking, secure messaging, and some OEM apps — limit what third-party listeners can see. Keynotif can detect that a notification arrived but may not access its full content.

🍎
Android only — no iOS

iOS does not expose notification listener APIs to third-party apps. Keynotif is built on Android's NotificationListenerService and cannot function on iOS. This is a platform limitation, not a roadmap item.
