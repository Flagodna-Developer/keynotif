Known Limitations

OEM Battery Optimization
Manufacturers like Xiaomi, OPPO, Realme, and Vivo aggressively kill background services. Keynotif includes onboarding guidance to whitelist the app. Without this, it may not run overnight reliably.

Notification Content Access
Some apps (such as banking or secure messaging apps) restrict notification content. Keynotif may detect the notification but cannot always access its full content.

No iOS Support
iOS does not provide notification listener APIs for third-party apps. Keynotif is Android-only.
